Our programs

Holistic, practical experiences that help learning feel safe, useful, and possible.

Inspired by SHOFCO’s idea of holistic, needs-driven services, this OKA4School page reframes your public program areas into a clearer school-community model that combines wellbeing, baking, and accessible STEAM.

Program logic

The current OKA4School site already presents three core strands — psychosocial support, practical baking, and low-cost STEAM — so this page turns them into a more complete programs view with room for future expansion.

This structure follows the same broad logic as SHOFCO’s programs presentation by showing connected services rather than isolated activities.

That framing fits what is already public on your site, where the initiative combines emotional support, practical life-oriented learning, and future-facing STEAM exposure in one portable model.

Impact model

Start with the learner, then widen the circle.

A ripple model works well here because the first point of change is the learner, then the group, then the school and community.

1

Learner

Build confidence, curiosity, and a sense of safety through hands-on sessions.

2

Classroom or group

Strengthen participation, teamwork, and visible engagement in the learning process.

3

School and community

Create trust, local support, and more pathways for collaboration and continuity.
