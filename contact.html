Trust and safety

A small legitimacy note protects the project.

SHOFCO uses its contact page to warn people about fraud and unofficial communication channels, which is a strong practice for any public-facing initiative.[page:1]

  • List your official website and official email domains clearly.
  • State that sensitive financial or password information will never be requested through unofficial channels.
  • Ask schools and partners to verify unusual requests directly through your public contact details.
