About us

Community-rooted learning that travels to where young people are.

OKA4School STEAM on Wheels is an emerging Kenyan community-based initiative bringing mobile wellbeing, baking, and STEAM learning experiences to schools and communities, especially where resources and access are limited.

OKA4School today

Like the SHOFCO About page, this section is structured around a clear identity statement, a mission-led framing, a set of priorities, and a practical description of where the work happens.

Mission

Our mission is to improve wellbeing, confidence, and future-readiness by turning everyday spaces into joyful, hands-on learning circles that weave together psychosocial support, baking, and accessible STEAM experiences.

Our priorities

A holistic approach to access, dignity, and possibility.

The SHOFCO About page organizes its identity through a concise “Our Priorities” section, so this version follows that same editorial pattern while adapting it to OKA4School’s current public mission and school-community focus.

Wellbeing first

Young people need safe spaces where emotional wellbeing, belonging, and confidence are treated as part of learning rather than separate from it.

Practical learning

Baking and low-cost STEAM activities make science, creativity, and problem-solving feel tangible, joyful, and locally relevant.

Inclusive access

The initiative is designed to reach learners where they are, especially girls and young people in under-resourced schools and communities.

Community partnership

Schools, families, local groups, and future partners all have a role in making learning circles sustainable and trusted.

Future pathways

The work connects everyday learning experiences to future pathways in STEAM, livelihoods, and self-belief.

Where we work

Starting locally, with room to grow.
