About us

A community-rooted idea for learning, dignity, and practical possibility.

SHOFCO’s About Us page introduces the organization through identity, mission, priorities, and place, and this page adapts that structure for OKA4School as a smaller, emerging initiative rooted in wellbeing, practical learning, and mobile STEAM access.[page:1]

Current positioning

OKA4School is presented here as a growing community-based learning initiative that connects psychosocial support, practical making, and future-facing STEAM in a portable format.

Mission

To make learning feel more human, more practical, and more reachable.

SHOFCO states its mission in terms of dignity, hope, and community power, and this adapted version gives OKA4School a similarly values-led statement focused on learners, families, and school communities.[page:1]

Our mission is to create mobile, community-rooted learning experiences that support wellbeing, strengthen confidence, and open up access to practical and imaginative STEAM opportunities for children and young people.

Our priorities

The values and working principles behind the model.

Wellbeing first

Learning is stronger when children feel seen, safe, and emotionally supported.

Practical learning

Hands-on work like baking and making helps ideas become concrete, social, and memorable.

Accessible STEAM

STEAM should feel possible even in low-resource settings, using simple tools and imaginative facilitation.

Community trust

Programs grow best when schools, families, and local supporters understand and shape the work together.

Dignity and participation

Children and communities should be treated as active participants, not passive recipients.

Portable growth

The model should be light enough to start small and strong enough to grow through partnerships.

Where we work

Start locally, then build with care.

SHOFCO explains where it began and how its geography expanded, and that same narrative pattern works for OKA4School by anchoring the story in Nairobi and nearby learning communities before future growth.[page:1]

OKA4School is currently framed as a Nairobi-rooted initiative with a mobile approach that can serve schools, families, and communities through pilot-based partnerships.

The immediate focus is not scale for its own sake, but building a credible starter route, documenting what works, and growing from evidence, trust, and local relevance.
